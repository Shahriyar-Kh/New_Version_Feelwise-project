Sad Mood Wellness Tips

Allow yourself to feel

It's okay to be sad. Don't rush to "cheer up"

Write it out

Journaling can help untangle your emotions

Cry if you need to

Tears are healing. Let them flow

Rest and comfort yourself

Wear cozy clothes, wrap up in a blanket.

Go outside, even briefly

Nature soothes. A walk, fresh air, or even just sitting under a tree

Talk to someone safe

You don't have to explain everything

Practice small acts of self-kindness

Drink water, eat nourishing food, say something gentle to yourself

Gratitude & Positivity

Write 3 things you're grateful for to shift focus.

Your feelings are valid. Be gentle with yourself today.
