Wellness Tips for Angry Mood

Pause Before Reacting

Take a short break before speaking or acting.

Deep Breathing

Inhale slowly through your nose, exhale gently.

Reframe Your Thoughts

Challenge negative assumptions with rational thinking.

Exercise Regularly

Even a quick walk lowers stress hormones.

Mindful Journaling

Write down what triggered your anger and how you felt.

Practice Forgiveness

Letting go reduces stress and rumination.

Quick Physical Release

Do pushups or stretch to release tension quickly.

Resolve Conflicts Calmly

Focus on solutions, not just venting.

Gratitude & Positivity

Write 3 things you're grateful for to shift focus.

Take care of your mental health. You matter.
